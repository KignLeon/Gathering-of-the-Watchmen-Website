World Prayer Targets

Strategic Intercession

Current Focus

Persecuted Believers Worldwide

Our ministry focuses strategic prayer on regions where the Body of Christ needs intercession — enforcing God's plan in the earth through proper prayer strategies.

Prayer Points

1

Pray for the protection of believers who face persecution for their faith.

2

Pray for bold witnesses to share the Gospel in regions where it is restricted.

3

Pray for the provision of Bibles and discipleship resources in local languages.

4

Pray for government leaders' hearts to be softened toward religious freedom.

5

Pray for healing and restoration among families affected by religious oppression.
